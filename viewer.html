What this project does

Predict drug resistance mutations before they appear in clinics

Three phases: learn structural signatures of known resistance hotspots across 13 TB genes, enumerate and score all accessible mutations by P(emergence), then validate prospectively in 12,287 CRyPTIC isolates and structurally with AutoDock Vina pocket-direct docking.

Model creation & training

Next steps

Now

Paper & presentation

Lead with gyrB Q538L. Use 9 other Vina-validated hits as pipeline benchmarks. Include CRyPTIC Tier 1 retrospective confirmations.

Phase 2

Extend to other diseases — MRSA first

Same pipeline architecture on Staphylococcus aureus (mecA, grlA/grlB, rpoB). MRSA is the first target pathogen.

Phase 3

Mantis platform integration

Deploy emergence forecasting inside the Mantis clinical genomics platform — Tier-4 alerts with structural validation and literature novelty flags at WGS interpretation time.
